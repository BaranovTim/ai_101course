Frequently asked questions

What exactly do I get when I buy a track?

Every course currently in the track, plus any course we add to it in the future — with quizzes, the AI tutor, and a PDF certificate per completed course. Access never expires.

Is the first lesson really free?

Yes — lesson one of every course, including its quiz and the AI tutor, is free with just an account. No card details needed.

How does the student discount work?

Upload a photo of your valid student ID in Settings. Our team reviews it, and once approved the reduced student price is applied automatically whenever you check out.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit and debit cards, processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.
